humanizing technical spaces.

intuition.

our tools should be human, an extension of our nature. crude and frusturating interfaces dehumanize. we should interact with our tools unconciously, integrated as much as possible.

austere.

the best component in no component. anything that does not add value, detracts value and distracts. minimal in form, maximal in focusing towards attention to where is should be.

functional.

comprehension of human value, drives efficiency. hoping more people spend less time in menial processes, and more time in creation and experiencing our world.
